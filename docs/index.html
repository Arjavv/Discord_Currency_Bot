Beginner & Setup Guide

A simple step-by-step documentation to configure the bot and start claiming your souls.

01

Invite & Setup

Add the bot using the link in the nav bar. As an Admin, execute s setup (prefix) or /admin setup (slash). This automatically configures the category containing #soul-bot (for gameplay/commands) and #soul-logs (private logs).

02

Milestones & Drops

Chatting actively in your channels builds progress milestones (10 messages = 100 Souls). Every 10 minutes, a random Soul Coin drops in the server drop channel. Players must type soul quickly in chat to claim the coin before it fades away!

03

Taxes & Upkeeps

A portion of all casino bets, flips, and user-to-user transfers are taxed and collected into the Server Bank. This bank fund is used to maintain your server’s premium bot services. Keep your server bank funded to avoid service decay.

Slash & Prefix Commands

A comprehensive list of bot commands to interact with the Soul Currency system.

s daily / /checkin User
Claim your daily allowance reward.

Triggers the 24-hour daily allowance checkin. Using the prefix command s daily (or aliases: s checkin, s claim) awards a random amount between 500 and 1,000 Souls. Using the slash command /checkin awards a fixed amount (default: 20 Souls). If run while on cooldown, displays a relative unlock timer embed.

Syntax: s daily or /checkin
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s cash [@username] User
Check your wallet balance or search for another user's balance.

Fetches the balance of Souls. You can optionally check another user's wallet balance by tagging/mentioning them. (Aliases: s cash, s balance, s bal, s money).

Syntax: s cash [@username]
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s lb User
View the top 10 richest users in the current cycle.

Displays an embed listing the top 10 richest users ranked in descending order of their current active Soul balances. (Aliases: s lb, s leaderboard, s rich).

Syntax: s lb
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s gift @user <amount> User
Send Souls to another user.

Transfers a specific amount of Souls from your wallet to another user's wallet. (Aliases: s gift, s give, s send, s transfer).

Syntax: s gift @username 100
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s rob @user Casino
Attempt a bank heist on another user.

Try to steal 10% of another user's wallet! There is a 30% chance of success. If you get caught (70% chance), you pay them a fine equal to 5% of your wallet. Both users must have at least 20 Souls. (Aliases: s rob, s steal, s heist).

Syntax: s rob @username
Cooldown: 1 hour per robbery attempt.
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s flip <heads/tails> <bet> Casino
Flip a coin for double or nothing.

Bets a specific positive amount of Souls on a coin toss outcome. Correct guesses award twice the bet amount (wins net your bet amount). Wrong guesses deduct the bet amount. Note: The coin flip is currently rigged to a 30% win chance. Defaults to heads if no choice is provided. Checks for balance sufficiency before running. (Aliases: s flip, s casino, s bet).

Syntax: s flip 100 (defaults to heads) or s flip tails 100
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s crash <bet> Casino
Watch the multiplier climb and cash out before it crashes.

Place a bet and watch the multiplier tick up in real-time from 1.00x. Hit the Cash Out button to secure your winnings before the game randomly crashes! Note: Game auto-crashes if you do not cash out within 15 seconds. Uses a house-edged probability curve capped at 10x multiplier.

Syntax: s crash 100
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s mines <bet> [mines_count] Casino
Interactive grid-based Mines game.

Stake-style mines game. Reveal tiles on a 4x5 button grid. Safe tiles (💎) increase your multiplier, while hitting a mine (💣) instantly crashes the game and loses your bet. You can cash out at any time after revealing at least one safe tile. Note: Auto-times out after 60 seconds of inactivity.

Syntax: s mines 100 (defaults to 3 mines) or s mines 100 5 (5 mines, range: 1-19)
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.

Fetches your Strength ⚔️, Defense 🛡️, Speed ⚡, and Magic 🔮 stats. Upgrades reset every Sunday midnight. To maintain complete privacy:

Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.

Browse training upgrades, elixirs, and Divine Shields.

Lists available boosts and item costs for your server. Weekly upgrades (+5) reset every Sunday midnight. Potion elixirs (+15) last for 24 hours. Includes an interactive dropdown list below the embed for quick direct purchases.

Directly purchase an item by specifying its ID (e.g., dumbbell, vest, shoes, tome, rage, aegis, adrenaline, mana, shield). (Note: Purchases can also be completed using the select menu dropdown in s shop).

s fight @user <bet> Casino
Challenge another player to a mystery stat clash showdown.

Initiate a wager duel against another player. The defender has 30 seconds to click Accept. The bot randomly selects a stat (Strength, Defense, Speed, or Magic) to compare. The player with the higher total stat wins the pot. Winner takes all, exact values for the clashed stat are revealed post-fight, and a death trigger automatically runs !kill <@loser> in the channel.

Syntax: s fight @username 500
Channel restriction: Runs ONLY in channels with soul-bot in their name.
s <admin-command> Admin
Prefix commands for server setup and drop management.

These prefix commands are restricted to members with the Administrator permission on the server. They can be run in any channel unless noted.

  • s setup: Automatically creates the "Soul" category with #soul-bot (public) and #soul-logs (private admin) channels.
  • s set-drop-channel [#channel]: Configures which channel random Soul Coin drops will spawn in. Defaults to the current channel if no argument is given.
  • s force-drop: Instantly forces a Soul Coin drop in the configured drop channel.
  • s reset-cycle: Archives current standings and resets all balances to 0. Restricted to #soul-logs. Disabled in Global Economy mode.
Permission: Server Administrators only.
/admin <subcommand> Admin
Slash commands for server setup, drops, and cycle management.

All /admin slash subcommands require the Administrator permission. These are the officially registered Discord slash commands.

  • /admin setup: Creates the Soul category and both bot channels if they don't already exist. Can be run anywhere.
  • /admin set-drop-channel [channel]: Sets the Soul Coin drop target channel. Defaults to current channel. Can be run anywhere.
  • /admin force-drop: Immediately triggers a Soul Coin drop in the drop channel. Can be run anywhere.
  • /admin auto-drops <start/stop>: Starts or stops the automated 10-minute drop cycle in the configured drop channel. Can be run anywhere.
  • /admin reset-cycle: Closes the current cycle, archives rankings, and resets all balances to 0. Restricted to #soul-logs. Disabled in Global Economy mode.
Permission: Server Administrators only.
Note: /admin reset-cycle must be run in #soul-logs. All other subcommands can be run from any channel.
soul User
Catch a Soul Coin drop.

Type exactly soul (case-insensitive) in the designated drop channel when a Soul Coin appears to claim its value. Be quick, drops expire after 5 minutes!

Syntax: soul
Cycle Archival System

Manual Monthly Reset Wipes

To keep server standings competitive and fresh, currency balances and chat counters do not persist forever. Administrators manually archive standings into snapshots at the end of each month before wiping active balances to 0 for a new cycle.
